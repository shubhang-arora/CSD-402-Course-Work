About Me

Hello World! My name is Shubhangi Kishore and I'm a final year Computer Science and engineering udergraduate student from Shiv Nadar University, India. My interests include Data Science, Machine learning and Algorithms. I've recieved Google's Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship and have encouraged participation of women in tech.

Programming has made me part of this lovely unbiased community dedicated to free distribution, propagation of knowledge and collaboration on projects. I've gotten addicted to the joy of building things and getting something to work. This field gives you the power to make your ideas evolve into real world solutions and teaches you how to deal with failures. The sheer possibilities of AI and deep learning and the impact it could have on millions of lives makes me want to contribute to this field.
